Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

In the country in Central America bordered by Nicaragua to the north, and the Caribbean Sea to the northeast. In Provincia de Alajuela, Costa Rica.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any group of professionals who want to step up their game. Want to be more unified, cohesive, tight-knit and laser-focused, this is for you.

What size teams can you accomodate?

There is not a size limit that we have addressed, as of yet, that was too large. Of course, Costa Rica is only so large...We look forward to your attempt to challenge our facilities and our staff as we work to bring the cream to the top. We are at your disposal.

Is there really no wifi available?

We merely suggest that you be device free, so that you can get the full affect of the program. The more you give to the program, the more you will recieve. When you can let go and let the process take over, you will come away refreshed, invigor- ated, and become a team.
