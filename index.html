La Crue Bottling Co.
October 2018

Unplugged Retreat lives up to their claims. We had the opportunity to engage in any number of activities. From zip-lining through the tops of the rain forest, to para-sailing over the pristine waters of the Caribbean and Pacific Oceans, rappelling and rafting in the canyons throughout the region. They have something to offer to everyone, regardless of age, gender, cultural or professional backgrounds. They got our team involved with activities that allowed our individual strengths to manifest themselves as they excelled as a team.

By enjoying their adventures, our stresses and anxieties disappeared and we became a unit. The experts on staff were exceptional guides through the obstacles we faced and built our self-confidence and camaraderie. They also offered a more relaxed program to pamper those of us who just need to let it all go. Relaxing night-time activities included dancing, dining and tropical cocktails with a beautiful view of the stars. We are now a well-oiled machine firing on all cylinders. We give them 5 Stars and would recommend their program to any company wanting to up their game.
